Privacy Policy

for the Artal Instagram Automation App (CAB-IG)

1 · Introduction

Artal ("we", "our", or "us") operates the CAB-IG application which automates replies and basic profile functions on Instagram via Meta's Instagram Graph API. This page explains how we collect, use and protect information when you interact with the CAB-IG app.

2 · Information We Collect

  • Instagram account ID and public profile data
  • Direct-message content only when permission is granted
  • Facebook Page ID linked to your Instagram Business account
  • Technical logs (IP address, time stamps) for security

3 · How We Use Information

Data is used solely to:

  • Generate automated DM replies through our VPS-hosted chatbot
  • Display basic profile data in our internal dashboard
  • Maintain and improve service stability

4 · Storage & Sharing

All data is stored on encrypted volumes on our private Ubuntu VPS located in the EU. We do not sell or share data with third parties. Access is limited to authorised staff under NDA.

5 · User Control

You may revoke CAB-IG's permissions at any time via Instagram or Facebook settings (Settings › Security › Apps and Websites). Once revoked, we immediately lose API access to your data.

6 · Data Retention

Logs are retained for up to 30 days for troubleshooting, then deleted automatically. Message content is cached only long enough to craft a reply (typically <1 minute); no long-term storage takes place.

7 · Security Measures

• All traffic is forced over HTTPS
• Access tokens stored using AES-256 encryption
• Server access protected by SSH keys and 2-factor authentication

8 · Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any major change will be announced on this page with a new "Effective Date".

9 · Contact Us

For questions about privacy, contact us at support@artal-eg.com
